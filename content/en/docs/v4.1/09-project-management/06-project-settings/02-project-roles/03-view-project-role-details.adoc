include::../../../../_custom-en/projectManagement/projectManagement-oper-openProjectPage.adoc[]

include::../../../../../_ks_components-en/oper-navigate.adoc[]

+

. Click the name of a project role in the list to open its details page.

. On the **Permissions** tab on the right side of the details page, you can view the permissions associated with the project role.

. Click the **Authorized Users** tab on the right side of the details page to view users with the current project role.
