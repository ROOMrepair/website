:ks_permission: **Cluster Management**

This section explains how to remove tags from a cluster.

== Prerequisites

include::../../../../_custom-en/platformManagement/platformManagement-prer-requiredPermission_v4.adoc[]


== Steps

include::../../../../_custom-en/platformManagement/platformManagement-oper-logIn.adoc[]

+

. Click **Cluster Management**.

+

include::../../../../_custom-en/platformManagement/multiclusterManagement/clusterTags/clusterTags-openListPage.adoc[]

+

. Click image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/more.svg[more,18,18] next to the tag you want to remove, then select **Remove from Clusters** from the dropdown list.

. In the **Remove Tag from Clusters** dialog, click image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/chain-off-duotone.svg[chain-off-duotone,18,18] next to the cluster from which you want to remove the tag, then click **OK**.
